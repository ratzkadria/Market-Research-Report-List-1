<p><p>The fragrances and perfumes market encompasses various types, including Eau de Toilette, Parfum, and Solid Perfume. Eau de Toilette usually contains 5-15% fragrance oils, providing a light scent ideal for daily wear. Parfum, also known as extrait or pure perfume, offers a higher concentration of 15-40% fragrance oils, resulting in a longer-lasting and more intense aroma. Solid perfumes feature fragrance oils in a balm or wax form, offering a portable and mess-free application, often favored for travel convenience.</p></p>
