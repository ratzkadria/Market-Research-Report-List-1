<p><strong>The Foot Operated Faucet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Foot Pedal</li><li>Pressed Foot Pedal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foot-operated faucet market consists of two primary types: contact foot pedals and pressed foot pedals. Contact foot pedals activate water flow through a physical touch, often requiring the user to step on the pedal, making them ideal for hands-free use in restrooms or kitchens. Pressed foot pedals, on the other hand, require sustained pressure to maintain water flow, allowing for a continuous stream while hands are occupied. Both types promote hygiene and convenience in various environments.</p></p>
